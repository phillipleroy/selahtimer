# Selah Timer Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 2026**

## Overview

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how Selah Timer handles your information.

## Data Collection

Selah Timer does **NOT**:
- Collect any personal data
- Track your usage
- Require an account
- Connect to the internet
- Share any information with third parties

## Data Storage

The only data stored by Selah Timer is:
- Your meeting start time preference
- Your assignment configuration (type, duration, start time)

This information is stored **locally on your device only** and never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services

Selah Timer does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Selah Timer does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL.
